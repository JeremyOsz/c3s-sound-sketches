Noise Percussion

Hits shaped by a triggered AD/AR envelope, with selectable source: white noise or Perlin sampled loop. Adjust hit rate, AD vs AR behavior, sequence pattern, attack time, and attack curve (linear, exponential, or logarithmic) to hear and see how onset shape changes the percussion character.

Controls guide (what each control does)
Start / Stop
Starts/stops the percussion engine. Browsers require a user gesture before audio will play.
Source
Chooses the raw sound: noise or a looped Perlin-like buffer.
Hit rate (Hz)
How often hits are triggered.
Sequence
A simple step pattern that gates which hits fire.
Envelope
Choose whether the hit uses AD or AR behavior.
Attack (ms)
How quickly the hit ramps up from silence.
Attack curve
Changes the shape of the onset (linear vs curved).
Decay/Release (ms)
How quickly the hit fades out after the attack.
