Sample & Hold frequency steps

We sample a noise signal at a regular rate and hold that value to drive an oscillator's pitch. Slow rates give stepping R2‑D2 tones; adding a quantiser snaps steps to musical notes.

Controls guide (what each control does)
Start / Stop
Starts/stops the synth. Browsers require a user gesture before audio will play.
Base pitch
The center pitch the sample-and-hold will wander around.
Range
How far above/below the base pitch the held values can push.
Rate
How fast new random values are sampled and held (in Hz).
Quantise to major scale
When enabled, snaps the stepped pitches to notes in a major scale so the output is more “musical”.
